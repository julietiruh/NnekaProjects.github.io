Business Case for Chimonica Bank: Loan Processing System Improvement

I developed a business case for Chimonica Bank to address delays in their loan approval process, which resulted in customer dissatisfaction and operational inefficiencies. The bank's manual, paper-based approach led to frequent errors and slowed decision-making, impacting its competitiveness. In my analysis, I identified key pain points and recommended the implementation of an automated loan processing system. This solution aimed to streamline operations, reduce errors, and improve overall customer experience, ultimately enhancing the bank's competitive position in the market.
